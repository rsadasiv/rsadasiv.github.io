OOPStreamViewer

Title: OOP Annotation Stream Viewer
URI: OOPStreamViewer
Routing: servlets/OOPStreamViewer.java -> jsp/OOPStreamViewer.jsp -> js/OOPStreamViewer.js
Parameters: Corpus, Document, Annotation
Content type: text/html

Horizontal bar chart visualization of sentence level annotations.
Mouse-over the bar to see the text of the sentence.
Click the bar to see the text of the sentence in context.
Mouse-over anywhere in the chart activates the vega-embed action menu (circle with three dots on far right).
